活動簡介｜About the Forum

在人工智慧與先進技術重塑全球產業的時代，半導體產業位居變革前線，是推動創新與進步的核心動力。當前對於高度專業技術人才的需求前所未有地迫切。本次論壇旨在強調培育因應挑戰、推動半導體產業持續發展之關鍵人才的重要性。

In an era where artificial intelligence and advanced technologies are reshaping industries worldwide, the semiconductor sector stands at the forefront, powering innovations and driving progress. The demand for a highly skilled workforce in this field has never been more pressing. This seminar aims to highlight the significance of cultivating talent that can meet these challenges and propel the semiconductor industry into the future.

主要議題｜Key Topics

人工智慧時代的半導體人才重要性：
隨著人工智慧驅動現今多數科技進展，半導體所扮演的角色愈加關鍵。與會講者將探討半導體創新如何實現 AI 技術能力，以及為何培育該領域人才對持續技術進展至關重要。

教育策略與產學合作：
了解學界與半導體產業如何合作發展教育課程、實習與訓練機會，以培養學生與專業人士具備應用於實務的能力。

全球人才發展的挑戰與機會：
探討全球在半導體人才培育上的整體布局，包括各地區所面臨的挑戰與可能創造一致性與高素質全球人力的潛力機會。

未來技能與人才準備：
掌握半導體專業人員日益重要的新興技能與知識領域。與會講者將說明如何保持競爭優勢，確保人才具備迎接未來科技發展的準備。

The Importance of Semiconductor Talent for the AI Era:
With AI driving much of the technological advancements today, the role of semiconductors is ever more critical. Our speakers will discuss how semiconductor innovations are enabling AI capabilities and why nurturing talent in this space is crucial for continued progress.

Educational Strategies and Industry Collaboration:
Learn about the collaborative efforts between academia and the semiconductor industry to develop education programs, internships, and training opportunities that prepare students and professionals for real-world applications.

Global Challenges and Opportunities in Talent Development:
Explore the global landscape of semiconductor talent cultivation, including the challenges faced by different regions and the potential opportunities to create a cohesive and skilled global workforce.

Future Skillsets and Workforce Preparedness:
Discover the emerging skills and knowledge areas that are becoming essential for professionals in the semiconductor industry. Our speakers will discuss how to stay ahead of the curve and ensure workforce readiness for future technological developments.

活動簡介｜About

半導體是全球戰略科技的核心動能，人工智慧、量子計算與新材料的快速突破，正重塑世界科技競局。

作為系列論壇的壓軸場次，本活動將鏈結臺、美、日、韓的產學研領袖與史丹佛博士後研究人才，共同探討半導體、量子、材料創新與跨領域應用的最新趨勢，並交流人才循環、國際合作與技術轉譯的實務策略。

這不僅是一場知識分享，更是打造臺灣未來科技戰略優勢的重要平台。

Semiconductors are the core driving force of global strategic technology, and the rapid breakthroughs in artificial intelligence, quantum computing, and new materials are reshaping the global technology landscape.

As the finale of the series forum, this event will connect industry-academia leaders from Taiwan, the US, Japan, and South Korea with Stanford postdoctoral research talents to jointly explore the latest trends in semiconductors, quantum, material innovation and interdisciplinary applications, and exchange practical strategies for talent circulation, international cooperation and technology translation.

This is not only a knowledge sharing session, but also an important platform for building Taiwan's future technological strategic advantages.

主要議題｜Key Topics

  • 聚焦全球半導體與量子科技趨勢：洞察材料創新、感測應用與 AI 驅動的新興領域
  • 跨國對話，亞洲連線：邀請臺、美、日、韓科技智庫與產學專家，剖析全球產業脈動
  • 博士後人才分享：國科會補助史丹佛博士後研究員分享矽谷前沿研究與臺灣應用潛力
  • 跨界交流：推動產學協作、商業媒合與導師制度，深化人才循環與國際鏈結
  • 政策呼應：呼應「人工智慧島」願景與量子國家計畫，探索臺灣科技佈局新藍圖
  • Focus on Global Semiconductor and Quantum Technology Trends: Insights into material innovation, sensing applications, and AI-driven emerging fields
  • Cross-national Dialogue, Asian Connection: Inviting technology think tanks and industry-academia experts from Taiwan, the US, Japan, and South Korea to analyze global industry dynamics
  • Postdoctoral Talent Sharing: NSTC-funded Stanford postdoctoral researchers share Silicon Valley cutting-edge research and Taiwan application potential
  • Cross-boundary Exchange: Promoting industry-academia collaboration, business matching, and mentorship systems to deepen talent circulation and international connections
  • Policy Response: Responding to the "AI Island" vision and quantum national plan, exploring Taiwan's new technology layout blueprint

注意事項｜Important Notes

一、活動參加辦法

  • 本活動採「免費預先線上報名制」，請確實留下正確資料。主辦單位保留報名審核權。
  • 報名截止日請見報名頁面，主辦單位得視報名狀況調整報名截止時間。
  • 報名後若無法參加，請指派其他人選並提前通知主辦單位。
  • 本活動提供與會者登錄「公務人員終生學習時數、研習證明」服務。

二、審核與通知

  • 經審核通過者，主辦單位將於活動前以電子郵件方式寄送「出席通知信」至您報名時所填電子信箱，以作為報到憑證。
  • 若報名審核未通過，將不另行通知。

三、報名須知

  • 請勿冒用或偽造他人身份進行報名，違者將依法處理。
  • 主辦單位保留報名資格之最終解釋與變更權利，恕不另行通知。

四、取消報名

  • 需取消報名者，請於活動3日前來電或 Email 通知。活動當日恕不接受取消，且未出席者不提供活動簡報檔。

五、講義與智慧財產

  • 活動結束後，凡已完成填寫活動滿意度調查問卷者，主辦單位將電子郵件方式提供論壇議程簡報講義。惟因智慧財產權保護，倘若外部講師未授權提供，該講師之講義將不予提供下載。
  • 為保護智慧財產權，嚴禁攝影及錄影等行為。

六、特殊情況

  • 主辦單位保留修改本活動規則之權利，毋須另行作出解釋或通知。
  • 本次活動若適逢天災(地震、颱風等)不可抗拒之因素，將延期舉辦時間另行通知。
  • 若因不可預測之突發因素，主辦單位得保留活動議程及講師之變更權利。

Important Notes｜注意事項

1. Registration Method

  • This event adopts "free advance online registration". Please provide accurate information. The organizer reserves the right to review registrations.
  • Registration deadline is shown on the registration page. The organizer may adjust the deadline based on registration status.
  • If unable to attend after registration, please designate another person and notify the organizer in advance.
  • This event provides "Civil Service Continuing Education Hours and Training Certificates" for participants.

2. Review and Notification

  • Approved applicants will receive an "Attendance Notification Email" sent to the email address provided during registration before the event, which serves as the check-in credential.
  • Those whose registration is not approved will not be separately notified.

3. Registration Guidelines

  • Do not impersonate or forge others' identities for registration. Violators will be dealt with according to law.
  • The organizer reserves the final interpretation and modification rights for registration qualifications without separate notice.

4. Cancellation

  • Those who need to cancel registration should notify by phone or email 3 days before the event. Cancellation on the event day is not accepted, and no presentation files will be provided to no-shows.

5. Materials and Intellectual Property

  • After the event, participants who complete the satisfaction survey will receive forum presentation materials via email. Due to intellectual property protection, materials from external speakers who do not authorize sharing will not be provided for download.
  • To protect intellectual property rights, photography and video recording are strictly prohibited.

6. Special Circumstances

  • The organizer reserves the right to modify these event rules without further explanation or notification.
  • If this event coincides with natural disasters (earthquakes, typhoons, etc.) or other force majeure factors, it will be postponed with separate notification.
  • Due to unpredictable emergencies, the organizer reserves the right to change the event agenda and speakers.
